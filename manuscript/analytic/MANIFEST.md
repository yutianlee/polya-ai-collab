one detailed analytic module; the main paper inputs its summary and the
supplement inputs its complete body directly.

## Build and publication

Run `./build.ps1`. It prefers `latexmk`/pdfLaTeX, compiles all three roots in
a fresh staging directory, checks source bytes and final logs, and publishes
the canonical and `output/pdf/` copies only after every root passes.

The flattened referee bundle is generated by
`../generate_submission_sources.ps1` and lives under
`output/submission/spherical-shell-polya-revision2/`. Its `SHA256SUMS.txt`
has SHA-256
`91bbace93640de38e40c9463e3e8a2b8d3818b017491606ceb1d08f9e4c5231a`.
The corresponding seven-file ZIP archive has SHA-256
`2be9227df69608344595ffeca9ef1a97eef6baad6fa89e724d51b883e5e581e5`.

Successful compilation is not the final gate: inspect the final logs, verify
source/PDF synchronization, and visually inspect rendered pages.
