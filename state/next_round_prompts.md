$$
I_{16}=\left[\rho_*,\frac78\right].
$$

theorem domain in $I_{16}\times[0,\infty)$, including all owned equality and
threshold faces. The canonical unresolved set is

$$
\boxed{
\mathcal D_{16}
=(I_{16}\times[0,\infty))\setminus\mathcal A_{16}.

$$

1. on $[\rho_*,1/16]$, a residual point can occur only for $K<64$;

## Round 17 objective

The primary objective is to prove $\mathcal D_{16}=\varnothing$ by analytic
or symbolic compression. If complete closure is not reached, the round may
promote only an exact, strictly smaller nonrectangular residual obtained from
a fully proved new analytic domain with every face assigned.

The secondary objective is limited to at most one rigorous face-connected
extension of the retained Round 8 pilot certificate. It must stay inside
$\mathcal D_{16}$ and remains subordinate to the analytic program.
