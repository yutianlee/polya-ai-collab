# General-dimensional shell project: next portfolio-discovery launch

## Purpose

Run a blind, diverse discovery phase before selecting the next
obligation-certification round.  This packet changes workflow only.  It does
not change any mathematical status in `state/proof_obligations.yml`.

This is a new human workflow directive following the Round 47 final audit. It
supersedes only that audit's assignment of component inequality $U$ as the
sole Round 48 obligation. All mathematical determinations, dependency
classifications, counterexamples, and no-status-change conclusions in the
Round 47 audit remain in force. The component inequality remains an active
registered route, but it is no longer the entire discovery search.

The completed three-dimensional shell theorem remains frozen as
`proved_internal`.  The general-dimensional theorem remains unproved.

$$
G_R(z)=\frac{\sqrt{R^2-z^2}-z\arccos(z/R)}{\pi}
$$

on $0\le z\le R$, with zero extension, put
$A=G_K-G_\mu$, $\mu=\rho K$, and

$$
q_a=[A(a)+1/4]_<,
\qquad
[x]_<:=\max\{0,\lceil x\rceil-1\}.
$$

In $d=4$,

$$
W_4=\frac{K^4-\mu^4}{64},
\qquad
P_4^{<}=\sum_{1\le a<K}a^2q_a.
$$

Resolve

$$
\boxed{\mathrm{WT}_4=W_4-P_4^{<}\ge0}
$$

on the complete strict active domain

$$
0<\rho<1,
\qquad
K^2>\frac{\pi^2}{(1-\rho)^2}+\frac34.
$$

A resolution means a complete proof or an exact counterexample.  Do not answer
merely that a broader Pólya problem is open.

## Root-agent operating rules

1. Read `state/approach_registry.md` and keep several incompatible families
   alive.
2. Use the available subagent slots dynamically.  Do not assign a permanent
   number of agents to a route.
3. Give blind discovery agents this packet and only the audited dependencies
   needed for their family.  Do not give most agents the Round 47 incumbent,
   reviews, or the currently favored component inequality.
4. Freeze independent outputs before cross-pollination.
5. Redirect later waves toward underexplored mechanisms.  Reopen a blocked
   family only for a materially new invariant, representation, construction,
   or estimate.
6. Promote only a bounded, falsifiable new claim into the existing
   lead/clean-room/adversarial obligation workflow.
7. Keep discovery metadata out of mathematical status fields.

With four concurrent slots, use the root plus three independent subagents in a
wave.  A recommended first wave is:

- one direct attempt to prove or falsify $D_A(a)\ge0$ in $d=4$;
- one direct layer-cake or inverse-level-set proof of $W_4\ge P_4^{<}$ that
  does not pass through component inequality $U$; and
- one exact falsifier targeting the literal aggregate and the two proposed
  stronger routes.

The root synthesizes and updates the registry.  A later wave should rotate to
the component, kernel, discrete-majorization, generating-function, or
alternative-spectral families according to the evidence rather than repeating
the first assignments automatically.

## Required discovery output

Every agent must return:

1. approach-family ID and exact claim attacked;
2. a concrete identity, lemma, construction, proof attempt, or exact
   counterexample;
3. dependencies actually used;
4. an exact loss ledger;
5. the first unsupported implication;
6. literal-target and route-specific falsification checks; and
7. one recommendation: continue, block, redirect, or promote.

Vague optimism, literature-status answers, and a reduction ending in an
equivalent unnamed positivity claim are insufficient.

## Mandatory adversarial faces

Check strict action walls, integer and near-integer $K$, integer and
near-integer $\mu$, truncated final cells, the inner interface, the strict
active wall, moderate frequency, $\rho\downarrow0$, $\rho\uparrow1$, exact
branching weights, and double counting of bonus or neighboring-tail payments.
Computation is diagnostic unless it is separately certified for an explicit
finite obligation.

## Completion boundary

Discovery may report exact gaps and counterexamples.  It must never promote
$\mathrm{WT}_4$, the all-dimensional aggregate, or the spectral theorem.
Promotion occurs only after a candidate passes the repository's existing
obligation-certification protocol.
