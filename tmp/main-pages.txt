     Scope and status. This manuscript proves a theorem for the class of genuine three-
     dimensional spherical shells. It does not claim the general Pólya conjecture, literature
     novelty, or priority. All shell-speciﬁc analytic reductions, regional arguments, seam as-
     signments, exact constants, and all ﬁnite exact-rational decisions are stated in this paper
     and its accompanying purely analytic supplement. Executable replay sources and in-
     terval certiﬁcates are retained only as optional regression checks and are not premises
     of the proof. The imported background and analytic results comprise explicitly iden-
     tiﬁed standard Sturm–Liouville and Bessel facts and published phase, Bessel-zero, and
     one-dimensional ﬂoor-sum inequalities, each quoted with its precise scope.

     The proof is purely analytic. It separates variables, converts the radial spectrum into a
     strict multiplicity-weighted phase count, and combines analytic endpoint estimates, ﬁnite

     remainder estimate on the ﬁnal compact window, and an analytic curvature propagation

     by congruent rigid-motion copies with disjoint interiors, under either exact or almost-
     everywhere coverage.
