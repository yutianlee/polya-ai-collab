                                                   Abstract
         This note replaces the exact-executable arithmetic used in the high spherical-shell staircase by
     displayed rational witnesses. It contains the complete seventeen-face localization registry, the three
     algebraic splits, the three dashed (impossible) cap cells, the ﬁve global checkpoint payments, the seven
     conditional payments, and the corrected cap–74 path. All comparisons are strict rational comparisons
     after the two elementary bounds 333/106 < π < 22/7. In particular, no decimal approximation,
     interval package, or external ledger is a logical input to this packet.
