                   A Purely Analytic Aggregate-Tail Theorem


                                                  Abstract
          We assemble three independently proved analytic base-frequency inequalities with the curva-
      ture propagation from the spherical-shell argument. The result replaces the aggregate interval
      certiﬁcate: for 7/51 ≤ ρ ≤ 39/50 and K ≥ 200, the continuous reserve is strictly positive
      and the discrete aggregate implication proves the Dirichlet Pólya inequality. The inclusion
      ρc = 1/(1 + 2π) > 7/51 then gives the exact ratio range required in the main proof. No interval
      enclosure, numerical sign test, or certiﬁcate output is used.
