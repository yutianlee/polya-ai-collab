3     The ﬁve delicate interfaces
For clarity, the ﬁve extra assertions formerly checked separately by the executable ledger are con-
sequences of the displayed sets:
    1. At ρ = ρ0 , the identity (2ρ0 )−1 = d makes the prospective second lower ﬁbre empty; the ﬁrst
       set in (1) owns the full equality ﬁbre, and Lown removes it exactly once.
    2. The face ρ = ρc belongs to Dhigh . It is not an optical face, so points with k11 (ρc ) < K < U (ρc )
       remain in D20 .
    3. The face ρ = ρopt = 39/50 belongs to Oown and is therefore absent from D20 .
    4. The frequency face K = k11 (ρ) belongs to Sown , whereas K = U (ρ) is excluded already by
       the strict inequality in D19 .
    5. If ρc < ρ < ρopt and k11 (ρ) < K < U (ρ), the point is in Dhigh but in none of Lown , Sown , Oown ;
       hence it survives, and every surviving point has exactly these properties.
