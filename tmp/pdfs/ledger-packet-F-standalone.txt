                                                Abstract
          The executable exact ledger checks a 9 × 7 truth table when the accepted lower, staircase,
      and optical regions are subtracted from the Round–19 residual. This note replaces all sixty-
      three state checks and the ﬁve additional interface checks by one set-theoretic calculation. The
      surviving set is then placed directly inside the closed domain of the all-frequency retained-
      remainder theorem. Every open and closed face is retained explicitly, and no artiﬁcial frequency
      interface is introduced.

3     The ﬁve delicate interfaces
For clarity, the ﬁve extra assertions formerly checked separately by the executable ledger are con-
sequences of the displayed sets:
    1. At ρ = ρ0 , the identity (2ρ0 )−1 = d makes the prospective second lower ﬁbre empty; the ﬁrst
       set in (1) owns the full equality ﬁbre, and Lown removes it exactly once.

    2. The face ρ = ρc belongs to Dhigh . It is not an optical face, so points with k11 (ρc ) < K < U (ρc )
       remain in D20 .

    3. The face ρ = ρopt = 39/50 belongs to Oown and is therefore absent from D20 .

    4. The frequency face K = k11 (ρ) belongs to Sown , whereas K = U (ρ) is excluded already by
       the strict inequality in D19 .

    5. If ρc < ρ < ρopt and k11 (ρ) < K < U (ρ), the point is in Dhigh but in none of Lown , Sown , Oown ;
       hence it survives, and every surviving point has exactly these properties.

Proposition 2 (Direct ownership of the exact residual). One has

                                     D20 ⊂ Rrr ,        D20 \ Rrr = ∅.                         (13)

Consequently the strict Pólya inequality holds at every point of D20 .

Proof. If (ρ, K) ∈ D20 , then

                                ρc ≤ ρ < ρopt   and k11 (ρ) < K < U (ρ).

The ﬁrst pair of inequalities implies ρc ≤ ρ ≤ ρopt , and the second implies K ≥ k11 (ρ). Hence
(ρ, K) ∈ Rrr , which proves (13); the ﬁnal assertion follows from (12).
    The inclusion does not change any inherited owner. The face ρ = ρopt and the frequency face
K = k11 (ρ) are outside D20 , although the stronger theorem also proves them. The face K = U (ρ)
remains excluded from D20 , while ρ = ρc remains included subject to the two strict frequency
inequalities. There is no additional frequency face.
