                                            Abstract
         This support volume contains the detailed hand proofs and exact-rational tables used
     to remove every executable predicate from the logical dependency chain of the spherical-
     shell theorem. Parts I–VI retain the previously audited 611-row exact ledger, including
     superseded rows as optional cross-checks; Parts VII–IX prove the retained-remainder
     theorem for every frequency above the final staircase; and Part X assembles the final
     residual closure. The former aggregate-tail argument, programs, and interval certificates
     remain available only as optional independent regression checks and are not premises of
     this analytic proof.

Reading convention. The volume is modular so that each bottleneck lemma can be
reviewed and hashed independently. Every included part is generated from its corresponding
LaTeX source in the manuscript/analytic directory. The repeated title pages deliberately
mark the trust boundary between exhibits.
