Purely Analytic Closure of the Final Spherical-Shell Residual
Abstract
This note assembles the all-frequency retained-remainder estimate after the exact owner
subtraction. It proves that the ﬁnal spherical-shell residual is empty without invoking an interval
certiﬁcate, an executable truth table, an aggregate tail argument, or a frequency split. The
structural, angular-block, and scalar-positivity modules cover one closed superset of the entire
residual.
